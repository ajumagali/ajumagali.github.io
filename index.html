About Me

I am Asyl, and I work for Z端hlke Engineering Ltd as a software engineer. I have 10 years of experience in related job positions. I started working as a software engineer in a small company moving to a teaching/lecturing at university.

In 2015, I came to the UK to pursue a PhD degree in Computer Science at Lancaster University. My research was about applying model-driven engineering techniques to facilitate implementation of multi-tenancy at the data layer. As an outcome of my research, I implemented a graphical domain-specific modeling language that allows designing and evolving a multi-tenant data architecture that consists of different cloud storage types.

I am a team player, outgoing, dedicated, and open-minded person. My passion is healthy life style and learning new languages. I get across to people and adjust easily to changes. I always work on developing my professional skills and learning new things all the time. My professional interests include software engineering, test-driven development, agile software development, cloud computing, DevOps, IoT, and model-driven engineering.
